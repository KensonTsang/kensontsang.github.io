About me

I am a software developer based in the UK, with a focus on creating mobile games, AR development, and interactive programs. With over 9 years of experience in Unity3D development and a total of 13 years in programming, I have honed my skills to become proficient in these areas.

I am truly passionate about software architectures and strive to write clean, maintainable, and scalable code. I adhere to SOLID principles, embrace test-driven development (TDD), and apply Domain-Driven Design (DDD) principles to achieve robust and high-quality software solutions.

In addition to Unity3D and C#, my solid programming foundation enables me to explore different engines and languages. I have experience with Unreal, C++, iOS development, Swift, and Python.

My primary expertise lies in programming and software development, but I am also deeply curious about gaming VFX design. I have dedicated myself to learning VFX design, enabling me to create Unity 3D VFX designs & models, particle systems, and shader programming.

I have also had the valuable experience of founding a Mobile Game company. This opportunity allowed me to oversee the entire game development process, from raising funding and game prototyping to game development and marketing.

When it comes to work, I excel both as an individual and as a team member. I leverage my experience to contribute to the overall success of projects, ensuring that goals are met and quality is delivered.

Beyond my technical skills, I possess strong problem-solving abilities and excellent communication skills. I particularly shine in new pitches or when translating technical terms to clients, making complex concepts accessible and understandable.
